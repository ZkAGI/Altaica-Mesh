# Acceptable Use Policy

Altaica Mesh exists to **protect legitimate, sensitive data** from untrusted infrastructure — so a
hospital, bank, law firm, or government team can run AI on a borrowed or remote GPU **without that
GPU being able to read the data**. That is the purpose, and it is the only purpose we endorse.

- an **ethical statement** of the project's intended use and prohibited uses, which we ask every user

- a **binding condition** of any hosted service, API, or deployment operated by the
  **ZKAGI Ecosystem Association** or its partners — using such a service constitutes agreement to
  this policy.

## Intended use (what this is for)

Privacy-preserving inference for **lawful, productive purposes in regulated or sensitive-data
contexts**, including but not limited to:

- **Healthcare / life sciences** (e.g. patient records, HIPAA / GDPR-special-category data)
- **Financial services** (e.g. client data, transactions, under applicable financial regulation)
- **Legal** (privileged or confidential client material)
- **Government / public sector** handling sensitive citizen data
- Any organization that must keep prompts and outputs confidential from the compute provider.

## Prohibited uses (what this must NOT be used for)

You must **not** use Altaica Mesh, or any service built on it, to:

1. **Break the law** — any unlawful purpose under applicable law, in any jurisdiction where you operate.
2. **Process, generate, or conceal illegal content** — including child sexual abuse material,
   content facilitating terrorism or violence, or other clearly illegal material.
3. **Evade lawful oversight or compliance** — the obfuscation must not be used to circumvent
   anti-money-laundering / KYC, sanctions screening, lawful audit, regulatory inspection, lawful
   intercept where legally mandated, records-retention obligations, or any duty you are legally
   bound to meet. **"The node is blind" is a privacy property, not a legal shield** — it does not
   relieve any party of responsibility for the data they process or the use they make of the system.
4. **Harm people** — unlawful surveillance, stalking, unlawful profiling or discrimination, denial
   of essential services, or generating targeted harassment, fraud, malware, or disinformation.
5. **Endanger safety-critical or weapons systems** — including development of weapons or attacks on
   critical infrastructure.
6. **Violate third-party rights** — including the license of any base model you scramble, or any
   applicable intellectual-property or contractual obligation.

## Responsibility

Privacy and legal responsibility are not the same thing. Altaica Mesh keeps a remote GPU *blind* to
your data; it does **not** make you, or any operator, exempt from the laws and regulations that apply
to that data and its use. **You remain fully responsible** for the legality of your data, your
prompts, your outputs, and your deployment.

This is not legal advice. For commercial deployment, have counsel confirm your use complies with the
laws and regulations of every jurisdiction in which you operate.
